I am a 2nd year PhD student at the [COMMEDIA](https://team.inria.fr/commedia/) team, a joint team between [LJLL](https://www.ljll.fr/) (Sorbonne Université) and [INRIA](https://www.inria.fr/fr/centre-inria-de-paris) (Centre de Paris), supervised by [Miguel A. Fernández](https://team.inria.fr/commedia/fernandez/) and [Guillaume Delay](https://www.ljll.fr/delay/).

My PhD thesis is titled **"Divergence-free finite elements for direct and inverse problems"**: my research focuses on the numerical analysis and implementation of a minimal low-order divergence-free finite element pair for incompressible flows, with stability and pressure robustness properties. I investigate its application in the context of unfitted mesh methods and fluid–structure interaction.

## Research Interests

- Numerical methods for incompressible flows
- Finite element methods
- Fictitious domain methods
- Fluid–structure interaction
